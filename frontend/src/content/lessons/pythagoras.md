## touch
### instruction
Grab the blue dot on side a and drag up to make it longer.

### insight
The hypotenuse c grew too. And look at the squares — the blue one (a²) got bigger, and so did the red one (c²). They always balance.

## squares
### instruction
Watch the squares. Blue area + amber area always equals red area. Drag both a and b to verify.

### insight
a² + b² = c². Always. No matter what size the triangle is.

## classic
### instruction
Set a = 3 and b = 4. What is c?

### insight
c = 5. The famous 3-4-5 triangle. Builders have used this for 4,000 years to make perfect right angles.
