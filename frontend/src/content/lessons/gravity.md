## touch
### instruction
Drag the distance between the bodies to see how gravity changes.

### insight
See the force arrow change? Closer bodies pull harder — gravity falls off with distance squared.

## distance
### instruction
Now drag the masses closer together. Watch the force arrow.

### insight
Halve the distance, quadruple the force. The r² in the formula is what makes gravity so strong up close.

## break-it
### instruction
Everything unlocked. Bring the masses as close as possible.

### insight
Huge masses + tiny distance = enormous force. This is black-hole territory.
