## touch
### instruction
Grab the blue dot and drag it along the curve.

### insight
The orange line tilts as you move. Its steepness IS the speed -- that's a derivative.

## secant
### instruction
Now drag h smaller. Watch the orange line approach the gray tangent.

### insight
As h -> 0, the secant becomes the tangent. That's what "limit" means -- just zooming in until two points become one.

## explore
### instruction
Find where the slope is zero -- the flat spots on the curve.

### insight
Slope = 0 at peaks and valleys. Setting the derivative to zero finds maximums and minimums. That's optimization.

## integral
### instruction
Toggle "Area" below. Drag t right and watch the shaded region grow -- that's the integral.

### insight
Derivative = distance -> speed. Integral = speed -> distance. They undo each other. This powers all of physics.
